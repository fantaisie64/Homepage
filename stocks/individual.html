Taiwan Stock Market Individual Broker


     For individual broker, we implement the feature of showing the edges (associations) when they are linked to one individual node (broker) when right clicking on it. That is, the visualization shows edges from/to one particular broker.

     In Figure 1, we shows edges from/to one individual broker in three levels (0.1, 0.01, 0.001) with discrete Blue, Green, and Red colors.
