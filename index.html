About me / About Wayne (Shu-Wei) Hsu

Wayne (Shu-Wei) Hsu (許書維) received M.S. in Electrical Engineering (Computer Science Group) at National Taiwan University in 2009. He was previously a software engineer in Quanta Computer, mainly working on Android application/framework design and development. He was also an assistant lecturer of iOS application development program in several institutions in Taiwan. He is presently a graduate student in Computer Science at UC Davis. He is now working with Professor Kwan-Liu Ma in ViDi (Visualization and Interface Design Innovation) research group.

His research interest spans the fields of Information Visualization, Computer Graphics, User Interface Design, Human-Computer Interaction, Mobile Computing and Web design.

    A Visual Approach for Name Disambiguation

    Production Line Data Visualization

    Co-authorship and Citation Network Visualization

    Taiwan Stock Market Transaction Analysis and Visualization
